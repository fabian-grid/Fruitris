How to Play

Aim: Align three matching fruits horizontally, vertically or diagonally to clear them.

Touch: Use the on-screen buttons or swipe left/right, swipe down to drop and tap the column to rotate.

Keys: ←/→ move, ↓ drop, space rotate, R restart.

Specials: 💣 clears all matching fruit below, 🔫 clears to the left, 🏹 clears diagonally up-left.
